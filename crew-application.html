Role Descriptions
Producer
Manages logistics, location scouting, scheduling, and keeps production on track. You're the backbone of the set.
Director of Photography (DP)
Operates camera, manages shot composition and lighting setup. Experience shooting narrative film or short form content.
Sound Mixer / Boom Operator
Records clean on-set audio. Must own or have access to basic sound equipment. Some equipment will be available on set. Experience on narrative sets a plus.
Production Assistant (PA)
General on-set support. No experience required — just reliability, a strong work ethic, and a willingness to learn.
Makeup
Handles talent makeup for camera. Must have your own kit and experience working under film lighting conditions.
Production Designer / Set Dresser
Builds and maintains the visual world of the film. Manages props, set details, and the overall physical aesthetic.
Composer
Creates original score or sound design for the film.
