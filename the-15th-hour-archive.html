Casting Postponed — Archived Post Original Casting Call · January 14, 2026

The 15th Hour

Short Film  ·  BLEUSKM Studios  ·  Director: Zaria Lashae

⚠ Production Update — April 2026

Production on The 15th Hour has been postponed due to persistent location availability issues and ongoing scheduling conflicts. This project is not cancelled — it is paused. Submissions for this casting call are now closed. A formal announcement will be made when production is ready to move forward.

BLEUSKM Studios announced an open casting call for The 15th Hour — a contained psychological drama set over the course of a single retail shift.

The film follows a quiet employee moving through a routine workday alongside coworkers and customers, observing the subtle rhythms of the store as time passes. As the shift continues, small changes begin to reveal a deeper pattern about routine, presence, and what it means to be replaced.

Roles that were casting:

Diana — Supporting. A coworker who has learned how to survive within the system. Emotionally adjusted on the surface, with hints of exhaustion beneath her composure.

Floyd — Supporting / Non-Speaking. A new employee who silently takes Diana's place. The performance should feel practiced and emotionally neutral — absorbed into the system.

NPC Customer #3 — Day Player. A regular customer. Casual, natural conversation. One scene.
