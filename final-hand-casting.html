Casting Announcement: “The Final Hand”

Casting Opens May 2026  ·  Production Begins Summer 2026 (Exact dates TBA)  ·  BLEUSKM Studios

△
Submissions Close — May 29th, 2026
All applications must be submitted by this date

BLEUSKM Studios announces casting for The Final Hand, a symbolic psychological short written and directed by Zaria Lashae.

Set inside a suspended lounge space where time feels both present and eternal, the film follows a man who chooses to sit at a table already waiting for him. What begins as a simple game of blackjack slowly reveals itself to be something more ritual than recreation.

At the center of the film is High John, a composed and watchful dealer who understands the stakes long before the Player does.

The film blends metaphysical symbolism with grounded performance, using playing cards as visual metaphors for desire, temptation, ego, and consequence.

Tone and Visual Style

Controlled, minimal dialogue. Dim lounge lighting against stark spotlight contrast. Slow camera movement and psychological pacing. Symbolism embedded in card reveals.

Roles Currently Casting

High John (Lead)  ·  The Player (Lead)  ·  The Stranger (Supporting)

This project will shoot in an intimate single-location setting, with heavy emphasis on performance nuance and atmosphere.
