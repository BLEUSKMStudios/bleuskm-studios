Of Blood and Dominion

"Balance must be maintained."
FormatFeature Film
StatusIn Development
Directed byZaria Lashae
Written byZaria Lashae
GenreSci-Fi ThrillerPsychological ThrillerDystopianMystery

In a society divided by blood classification and conditioned to accept its order, a series of unexplained abductions begins targeting a specific group. When a young man starts experiencing fragments of a memory that isn’t supposed to exist, he pieces together his own abduction and return...uncovering a hidden pattern tied to those taken, and a truth buried within them that threatens the system before it silences him again.
