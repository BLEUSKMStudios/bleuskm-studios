Production Update: "The 15th Hour"

April 2026 By BLEUSKM Studios

After careful consideration, BLEUSKM Studios has made the decision to indefinitely postpone production on The 15th Hour.

This was not a decision made lightly. Over the course of pre-production, the project experienced a series of compounding setbacks — persistent location availability issues and ongoing casting conflicts that continued to push production dates back without resolution. Rather than force the film into a timeline it wasn't ready for, or compromise the quality and intention of the work, we chose to step back.

At BLEUSKM, every production is built around a commitment to doing the work right. The 15th Hour is a film that demands a specific environment, a specific energy, and the right conditions to exist the way it was written. Those conditions were not consistently available during this production window, and we refused to let that become the story of the film.

The decision to postpone was made to redirect focus toward the productions that are positioned and ready to move — including The Final Hand, which is currently in pre-production with casting underway, and Love Me Like This, which opens casting May 4th.

The 15th Hour will be revisited. The project is not cancelled — it is paused. When we are ready to move forward, a formal announcement will be made through this channel and across all BLEUSKM platforms.

To everyone who submitted, reached out, or followed this project from the beginning — thank you. Your interest in this story is part of what makes it worth returning to. We'll see you on the other side of it.

— Zaria Lashae
Founder & Director, BLEUSKM Studios
April 2026
