Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026

BLEUSKM Studios ("we," "us," or "our") operates bleuskm-studios.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, submit casting applications, purchase merchandise, or subscribe to our newsletter.

Information We Collect

  • Contact & Project Inquiries: Name, email, phone, and project details submitted via our contact form.
  • Casting Submissions: Name, email, phone, age, location, headshots, resumes, reel links, and any information you provide in your submission.
  • Newsletter Subscribers: Name and email address.
  • Shop Purchases: Payment and shipping information are handled directly by Shopify and Stripe. We do not store payment card data.
  • Usage Data: Standard server logs including IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

How We Use Your Information

  • To review casting submissions and contact selected applicants
  • To respond to project inquiries
  • To send casting call announcements and studio updates to newsletter subscribers
  • To process merchandise orders through Shopify
  • To improve our website

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers (Netlify for form hosting, Shopify/Stripe for payments, email service providers) solely to operate our services.

Casting Submissions

All casting submissions are kept confidential and reviewed only by BLEUSKM Studios and directly involved production staff. Submitted materials (headshots, resumes, reels) are used solely for casting evaluation and are not shared publicly.

Newsletter

You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email, or by emailing newsletter@bleuskm.com with the subject line "Unsubscribe." We will remove you promptly.

Cookies

We use session storage for cart functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at studio@bleuskm.com.
